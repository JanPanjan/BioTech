A natural DNA repair mechanism that fixes [[Double stranded breaks|DSBs]] by using a homologous DNA template (e.g. sister chromatid, introduced [[Donor DNA cassette|donor DNA]]). Crucial for gene edition to achieve targeted gene knock-ins, point mutations or large insertions. It's often less efficient than [[Non-homologous end joining]]
